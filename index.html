Solutions

Control your diabetes with a digital touch.

Introducing NOLO Wellness, an ecosystem of synergistic diabetes care devices that co-exist to support you in your day to day diabetes care.

NOLO Temp Sense

Small, attachable sensor that will alert your mobile device if your insulin goes above or below the recommended temperature range to ensure safe storage of your insulin at all times!

  • Life-saving alert system
  • Reusable
  • Bluetooth capabilities

NOLO Smart Portion Scale

Pocket sized, Bluetooth scale that will let you know the precise amount of grams of carbs on your plate for a given meal.

  • Simple and seamless
  • Pocket-sized
  • Bluetooth capabilities

NOLO Comfort Sense

Needleless blood glucose meter that will let you know whether your blood glucose levels are high, low, or in range.

  • Non-invasive
  • Sends readings to mobiel app every 5 minutes
  • Works 14 full days
  • Bluetooth capabilities

About Us

Alia Borroho and Sean Hodgins founded Devfluent Labs in the Spring of 2016, to close the knowledge gap in low-income diabetic populations worldwide. Devfluent Labs is a technology company bringing you Devfluent Labs, an ecosystem of medical devices to help people manage their diabetes effectively, feasibly and painlessly.

The team at Devfluent Labs set its sights on tackling pain points in the diabetes community and developing tools to improve the overall wellbeing of these individuals living with diabetes. The team began its quest to develop a group of synergistic diabetes care devices that all work seamlessly together, to improve the health of people living with prediabetes, type one diabetes, and type two diabetes.

Devfluent Labs designs its products with your comfort in mind. We believe that being more comfortable should start TODAY.
